Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a PDF?
Go to the "Post" tab in the bottom menu. Select your file, choose the category (Lecture Note or Past Question), and hit upload.
Is PeerPoint free?
Yes! PeerPoint is a student-led initiative designed to make academic resources accessible to everyone for free.
Can I delete my posts?
Currently, you can delete your profile photo. To remove a resource or forum post, please contact a moderator through the support email.
